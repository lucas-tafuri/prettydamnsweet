<p id="">For their annual Lung Summit, Dachi Sankyo wanted to create an immersive experience for guests to begin their journey into learning about non small cell lung cancer. Fabricating a set of 7 foot tall 3D sculpted lungs, PDS created a “Living Lung” experience. </p><p id="">The short film utilized 3D projection mapping and rear projection to create an animated sculpture taking viewers on a journey from diagnosis through treatment of NSCLC. It was so impactful, that the piece has become a permanent installation in the DSI headquarters learning center.</p><p>‍</p>

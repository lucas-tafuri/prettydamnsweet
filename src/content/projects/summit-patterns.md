<p id="">Adobe Summit is a celebration of experience makers. Our opening film explores the beautiful balance of complexity and simplicity within a vibrant world of evolving creativity. The style approach was amixed-media blending 2D Illustration with 3D toon shaded renders to create a seamless Integration of a variety of elements. This allowed us to add surprising moments of depth and complexity, shifting perspective of the camera and lighting in unexpected ways.</p><p id="">This project was one for the books. Due to COVID-19, the event pivoted from a physical + online event to digital-only. It wasn't a simple task to take our nearly finished design and motion intended for large (and very wide) screens and shift to a 16x9 web format Just a couple of weeks out from the show, but the Incredible leadership at PIX Production and Adobe made this transition happen with ease.</p><p id="">We couldn't be more proud to be a part of this project or more excited to share this opening film with the world.</p>

<p id="">The concept was based around the show’s identity key art, which was a curved form broken apart into slices. There was something very interesting about the rigid breaks of a smooth organic form, so we created a journey through various worlds with this central theme in mind. The result is a mixture of styles and disciplines from hand drawn cell animation to photo-real CGI. Another wonderful collaboration with our friends at Adobe and PIX&nbsp;Productions!</p>

They splash onto the screen, change shape and pattern when hit with light, and evolve in scale and dimension over the course of the film. Along the way, we see them spread out, gather, grow in numbers, combine to form solid ideas, and evolve into new ideas. The story finally leads to insight as we land prominently in the Adobe Summit endtag overlooking the wondrous Las Vegas strip, all imagined in the style of these shiny colorful pixels.</p><p id=\"\">‍</p>"

<p id="">Our friends at Intrepid asked us to help them develop a concept for an immersive activation to take place inside of the Venetian hotel in Las Vegas during the 2020 CES event. Our team of artists, technical directors, and creative directors worked closely with Intrepid for months developing art direction, styleframes, pre-visualizations and curation of well-established artists that led to the final result. </p><p id="">The activation features data sculptures by media artist Refik Anadol, who created the sculptures using Intel data sets.</p><p>‍</p>
